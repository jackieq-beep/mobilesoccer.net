Privacy policy

Last updated: January 2026

Mobile Soccer Media respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you visit mobilesoccer.net or contact us through the site.

This website is intended for informational and business partnership purposes only. We do not operate consumer accounts, logins, or player profiles on this site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Information we collect

Information you provide voluntarily

When you contact us through email or a contact form, you may choose to provide information such as your name, email address, company name, and the contents of your message. Providing this information is voluntary and is used only to respond to your enquiry.

Automatically collected information

We may collect limited, non identifying usage data to understand website performance and reliability. This may include pages visited, approximate geographic region, device and browser type, and referral source. This data is aggregated and is not used to identify you.

How we use information

  • To respond to partnership and business enquiries
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the website
  • To understand website usage trends at an aggregate level
  • To maintain security, stability, and performance

We do not use personal information for advertising, profiling, or automated decision making.

Cookies and analytics

mobilesoccer.net may use standard analytics tools to measure performance and usage trends. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies to collect anonymous, aggregate data. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be processed by trusted service providers strictly for operating and maintaining this website, such as hosting, analytics, and email services used to respond to enquiries. Service providers are permitted to use information only to provide services to us and are expected to safeguard it.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to respond to enquiries or meet legitimate business or legal requirements. Information that is no longer needed is deleted or anonymized.

Your rights and choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to request access, correction, or deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

International visitors

mobilesoccer.net is operated globally. If you access the site from outside the country where our servers or service providers are located, your information may be processed across borders in accordance with this policy.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The updated version will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
